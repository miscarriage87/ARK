# ARK Digital Calendar - User Guide

## Welcome to ARK! 🌅

ARK is your personal digital calendar that delivers AI-generated, personalized quotes every day. Think of it as a modern twist on the classic tear-off calendar, but one that learns your preferences and grows with you.

## Getting Started

### 1. First Visit - Create Your Profile

When you first open ARK, you'll be greeted with a simple 5-question questionnaire. This helps us understand your personality and preferences so we can personalize your daily quotes.

**The Questions:**
1. **What motivates you most?** - Helps us understand your core drivers
2. **How do you prefer to start your day?** - Influences the tone of your morning quotes
3. **What type of content inspires you?** - Guides the style and subject matter
4. **How do you handle challenges?** - Shapes quotes for difficult times
5. **What's your ideal mindset?** - Determines the overall approach

**Tips for Better Results:**
- Answer honestly - there are no "right" answers
- Think about what truly resonates with you, not what sounds good
- Consider your current life phase and goals
- You can always adjust preferences later

### 2. Your First Daily Quote

After completing the questionnaire, you'll immediately receive your first personalized quote. This quote is generated specifically for you based on:
- Your personality profile
- The current theme (monthly and weekly)
- The day of the week
- Seasonal considerations

### 3. Providing Feedback

The key to improving your ARK experience is providing feedback on your quotes. For each quote, you can:

- **👍 Like** - You found this quote meaningful, inspiring, or relevant
- **😐 Neutral** - The quote was okay but didn't particularly resonate
- **👎 Dislike** - The quote didn't connect with you or felt off-target

**Why Feedback Matters:**
- ARK learns from every piece of feedback
- Your future quotes become more personalized
- The system adapts to your changing preferences over time
- Better feedback = better quotes

## Daily Usage

### Your Morning Routine

1. **Open ARK** - Visit the app or tap your installed PWA icon
2. **Read Your Quote** - Take a moment to reflect on your daily message
3. **Provide Feedback** - Let us know how the quote resonated with you
4. **Set Your Intention** - Use the quote to frame your day positively

### Quote Display

Each quote includes:
- **The Quote Text** - Your personalized daily message
- **Theme Information** - Current monthly and weekly themes
- **Date** - When this quote was generated for you
- **Feedback Options** - Like, neutral, or dislike buttons

### Navigation

- **Home** - Today's quote (main screen)
- **Archive** - Browse your quote history
- **Settings** - Adjust preferences and notifications
- **Profile** - View your personality profile and stats

## Features Guide

### 📚 Quote Archive

Your archive contains all your previous quotes, organized and searchable.

**Browsing Your Archive:**
- Quotes are displayed in reverse chronological order (newest first)
- Each quote shows the date, theme, and any feedback you provided
- Scroll to load more quotes automatically

**Search Functionality:**
- Use the search bar to find specific quotes
- Search by keywords, themes, or content
- Results are ranked by relevance
- Search terms are highlighted in results

**Filtering Options:**
- Filter by theme (monthly or weekly)
- Filter by date range
- Filter by your feedback rating
- Combine filters for precise results

### 🎨 Theme System

ARK organizes content around meaningful themes that change throughout the year.

**Monthly Themes:**
- Each month focuses on a specific area of growth or reflection
- Examples: "Personal Growth," "Relationships," "Creativity," "Resilience"
- Themes are designed to provide coherent periods of related inspiration

**Weekly Sub-themes:**
- Each week within a month has a related sub-theme
- Builds on the monthly theme with specific focus areas
- Provides variety while maintaining thematic coherence

**Seasonal Variation:**
- Themes change with the seasons and calendar
- Special themes for holidays and significant periods
- Ensures fresh content throughout the year

### 🔔 Notifications

Stay connected to your daily inspiration with smart notifications.

**Setting Up Notifications:**
1. Go to Settings → Notifications
2. Toggle "Enable Daily Notifications"
3. Choose your preferred time
4. Select your timezone
5. Choose whether to include quote previews

**Notification Features:**
- **Daily Reminders** - Never miss your daily quote
- **Preview Text** - See a snippet of your quote in the notification
- **Direct Access** - Tap to go straight to your quote
- **Smart Timing** - Respects your device's Do Not Disturb settings

**Best Practices:**
- Choose a time when you can reflect (morning is popular)
- Enable previews for quick inspiration
- Adjust timing if your schedule changes

### 📱 Mobile Experience (PWA)

ARK is designed as a Progressive Web App for the best mobile experience.

**Installing ARK:**
1. Open ARK in your mobile browser
2. Look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install" prompt
3. Follow your browser's installation steps
4. ARK will appear as an app icon on your device

**Offline Usage:**
- Recent quotes are cached for offline viewing
- Browse your archive without internet
- Provide feedback offline (syncs when online)
- Full app functionality even without connection

**Mobile Features:**
- Touch-friendly interface
- Swipe gestures for navigation
- Optimized for one-handed use
- Fast loading and smooth animations

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Quotes Not Loading**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache if problems persist

**Notifications Not Working**
- Ensure notifications are enabled in your browser/device settings
- Check that ARK has notification permissions
- Verify your notification time and timezone settings

**Personalization Seems Off**
- Provide more feedback to help the system learn
- Check your profile settings and update if needed
- Remember that personalization improves over time

**Sync Issues**
- Ensure you're using the same user ID across devices
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

**Performance Issues**
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Ensure you have sufficient device storage
- Close other browser tabs to free up memory
- Update your browser to the latest version
